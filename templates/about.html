About DANIFY

We're building the most reliable CBT platform for modern African education — purpose-built for examiners, schools and learners.

Home / About

Modern examinations, simplified.

ExamPro was founded in 2021 to fix the broken examination workflow in schools and tutoring centers. We saw paper-based exams slowing down learning, delaying results and limiting feedback.

Today we power hundreds of institutions across Nigeria with secure, fast and friendly online testing — from JAMB simulations to internal mock exams and continuous assessments.

Our Mission

To make high-quality assessment infrastructure accessible to every school, teacher and learner across Africa.

Our Vision

A future where every student gets fair, instant, data-driven feedback that helps them learn faster.
